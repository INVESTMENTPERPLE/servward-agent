Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Servward is a remote-control tool that lets you monitor and manage your own Mac from your iPhone. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information on our servers. The app has no backend infrastructure operated by EspymeLab. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services included.

2. Data stored on your devices

  • Server URL & bearer token — stored in the iOS Keychain and synced via iCloud Key-Value Store across your own devices. Never transmitted to any third party.
  • Custom commands — stored in iCloud Key-Value Store. Accessible only to you via your iCloud account.
  • APNs device token — sent once to the Mac server you set up and control, so it can deliver push alerts to your iPhone. Not shared with any third party.

3. iCloud

The app uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to sync your server settings and custom commands across your devices. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. EspymeLab has no access to your iCloud data.

4. Network communication

All communication is between your iPhone and the server URL you configure — a machine you own and operate. No data passes through EspymeLab servers because we do not operate any.

5. Push notifications

Push notifications are delivered via Apple Push Notification service (APNs). They originate from your own Mac agent and are routed through APNs to your device. EspymeLab has no access to notification content.

6. Third-party services

If you expose your Mac through Cloudflare Tunnel, your traffic passes through Cloudflare's network. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details. If you use Tailscale, see Tailscale's privacy policy.

7. Children's privacy

Servward is a technical tool intended for adults who manage their own computers. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
